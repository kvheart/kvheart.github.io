Getting started

When patient is first web enabled, he receives an email with subject Line “ Portal login information from your doctor's office”. It will come from Kensington Valley Heart with email address no-reply@eclinicalmail.com .

Patient should click the orange “Set up Portal Account” button in the email:

[note it will not work unless there is a phone number associated with the account]

He will see a page that will ask whether he want to receive a phone call or a text message with a verification code

He will then type the code from his phone into the website.

The website will then ask the patient to create a password

The website will then ask the patient to agree to consent form

After agreeing, patient will come to the patient portal

Signing in after account is set up

If you have already registered, you can sign in with either:

  1. Your Username and Password that you created when you first set up the account
  2. Your name, date of birth, and a verification code that will be sent to your phone.

This is the address to the portal website: https://health.healow.com/KVH

Go to the portal website, click “View your health record”:

On the next screen either choose “User Credentials” or “Using Mobile Phone” and then sign in.
